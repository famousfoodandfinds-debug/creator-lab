Getting Started
5 steps · read once · go generate
Welcome to Psych Lab
Everything you need to know before your first script.
1
The one idea behind everything
Psych Lab doesn't write about your product. It writes about your buyer -- who they are, what they feel, and what would make them stop scrolling and tap the cart.
Sell the person they become, not the product they buy.
2
Fill in both inputs
Every script starts with two fields. The first one is the most important thing you do in this tool.
Start here
When you chose this product...
Tell the tool about the buyer. Who are they, why did you choose this product, what does their life look like, what feeling are they chasing. The more specific you are, the better your scripts. This is not a product description -- it is your understanding of the person buying it.
Product details
Brand name, key features, specs. This becomes the proof in your body lines.
3
Pick a price range
Price shapes the buyer psychology behind every line -- an impulse buy script feels completely different from a premium upgrade script. Select the tier that matches your product before you generate. It is required every time.
4
Choose your mode
Four ways to generate depending on what you need.
🎯
Curated Script
Build one script step by step. Pick your hook, body lines, pre-close, and CTA one at a time.
⚡
Batch Scripts
Five complete scripts at once, each targeting a different buyer angle. Best when you need a full day of content fast.
🎬
Filming Session
One product, multiple formats -- unboxing, honest take, voice over, before and after, and more.
🎭
Skit Script
Two-person dialogue. Pick a relationship type and get a full conversation script.
5
Rate your lines
Thumbs up lines that sound like you. Thumbs down ones that don't. The tool learns your voice over time and adjusts every future generation to match.
